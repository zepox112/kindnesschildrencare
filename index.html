About us


We are a non-profit organization registered in Tanzania dedicated to rescuing orphans, street children, and children with disabilities from suffering and poverty. By providing education and holistic support, we revive the dreams and ambitions of those whose futures were threatened by parental loss or harsh life calamities. We believe these children are invaluable to our nation‚Äôs future if effectively empowered. KINDNESS CHILDREN CARE warmly invites volunteers and donors worldwide to join our movement. Let us not isolate this generation; show your compassion today and be a blessing to their lives. Under our Board of Directors, we remain committed to transforming the lives of vulnerable children living in distress both on the streets and in homes.
